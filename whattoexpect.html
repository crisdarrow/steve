What to Expect

When you call me, you will likely reach my voicemail. I will return your call within one business day.

I do a 10 minute initial free phone consultation. After this brief consultation, I usually schedule 3 sessions for a full assessment. If I see that I can serve you, we then proceed with therapy. Otherwise, I refer you to another therapist that might be a better fit for you.

Fees and Insurance

The fee is established between each client. I do not belong to any insurance panels nor do I charge insurance. I offer you a statement after each month that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement.

Payment

I accept cash or check as forms of payment.

Cancellation Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled appointment, and you have not notified me at least 24 hours in advance, you will be required to pay the full cost of your fee.

Approaches to Therapy

I assist families of teenagers to make the changes they need, so that their teenagers may begin thinking and acting like honorable young men and women.

I help you to identify and cope with triggers that are causing unhealthy behaviors and straining your relationships.

I help adults rediscover their purpose and passion, so that they may begin to act in alignment with them and be more effective in their personal and professional relationships.

I help couples discover each partner's contributions to their difficulties, and identify different choices each can make, so that together they may re-create the love, joy and fun they once shared together.

I work with a diverse population in the Santa Clara Valley especially individuals and families with cross-cultural issues.

I continue to work on my own development both personally and professionally.
