Adult Counseling Issues

Individual Issues

Do you find yourself burdened by the many decisions you have to make as an adult? How to perform well at school or work and also have a fulfilling life? It is no wonder that from time to time we can get stuck in a rut.

Despite all the ways you may have tried, maybe somehow you still manage to fall back. It may be time to try counseling to figure out how to keep from going backwards.

How I Help Adults

It may be time to try counseling to figure out how to keep from going backwards. I help adults who are stuck get unstuck. Having supportive and non-judgemental feedback can really be helpful to get you out of the rut and moving again. I help men and women use and trust the strengths in their character to make better decisions for themselves.

If you're considering counseling, you can predict that it won't be easy, but neither is dealing with the discomfort that motivated you to click on this page in the first place.

My compliments to you for having the courage to look for help today. You don't have to endure your difficulties and struggles by yourself.

Areas of specialty are:

• drug/alcohol issues

• anxiety/depression

• relationship problems

• men's issues (including husbanding)
